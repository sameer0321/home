Village History Research

The Village History Project, developed by the Documentation Center of Cambodia, is an effort to promote and Print the victims' memories. A workshop on strengthening research work was held to train 200 teachers. About how to compile the oral stories of the victims. All teachers will go to interview the victims who are living. All of this will lead to the establishment of a National Historical Commission. From the prevention of genocide in Cambodia, which continues to work on teacher training and the development of training materials for Educate about genocide.
